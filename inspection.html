Vehicle Inspection

Before you commit to purchasing or shipping a vehicle, GoSaSh provides a detailed inspection report with verified photos and videos.

Exterior Condition

Full walk-around photos of all panels, paint condition, dents, scratches, rust and frame look-over.

Interior Check

Dashboard, seats, electronics, AC, odometer, infotainment, and accessory verification.

Engine Start-up Video

Cold start, idle sound, warning lights, and basic mechanical impressions.

Optional Test Drive

If permitted by seller, short road test to evaluate shifting, braking and suspension behavior.
